RudderStack has launched RudderAI, an agentic layer that brings AI-powered workflows to the entire customer data lifecycle, enabling both technical and non-technical teams to use natural language to configure data pipelines, analyze customer segments, and activate insights across their tools. This represents a fundamental shift in how customer data platforms operate, moving from UI-locked systems to agent-ready infrastructure that reduces bottlenecks and accelerates time-to-insight.
